And Here's Why Most Color Mistakes Happen:

Reason 1:

Some weavers don’t understand that value is the most critical aspect of color because much of our visual system only perceives black and white.

Reason 2:

Some weavers don’t realize that your choice of draft often determines whether you wind up with bright or muddy colors.

Reason 3:

Some weavers pick the wrong scale for their woven pattern and also view it at the wrong scale when designing.

Reason 4:

Some weavers, not understanding color mixing, pick colors that blend into dull colors rather than bright ones.

Reason 5:

Some weavers become frozen in the face of color, thus inhibiting them from receiving any and all color help.
